About me

I am now a Ph.D. student at Michigan State University working with Prof. Yunhao Liu. I received my M.Eng degree from School of Software, Tsinghua University, supervised by Prof. Jiliang Wang and received my B.Eng degree from Beijing Jiaotong University. My research interests include the Internet of Things (IoTs), mobile computing, and network systems.
